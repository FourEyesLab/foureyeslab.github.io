Cosmic-CoNN

Chengyuan Xu, Curtis McCully, Boning Dong, D. Andrew Howell, Pradeep Sen

Abstract

Cosmic-CoNN is an end-to-end solution to help tackle the cosmic ray (CR) detection problem in CCD-captured astronomical images. It includes a deep-learning framework, high-performance CR detection models, a new dataset, and a suite of tools to use to the models
