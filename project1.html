Sample Project: A Cosmic Ray Detection Deep Learning Framework, Dataset, and Toolkit

Chengyuan Xu, Curtis McCully, Boning Dong, D. Andrew Howell, Pradeep Sen

Cosmic-CoNN is an end-to-end solution to help tackle the cosmic ray (CR) detection problem in CCD-captured astronomical images.

Images

3D visualization of the Median-Weighted mask. (a) A small image stamp that includes sources, CR affected pixels, and background. (b) Visualization of the CR-free me- dian frame shows inconsistency within the non-CR class as it could be further split into two sub-classes: sources and background. We want to down-weight the less useful yet dominant background pixels to prioritize learning to distin- guish between CRs and sources, but the brightness varia- tion between sources makes it hard to use the median frame as a weight mask directly. (c) The ground-truth CR mask shows the imbalance between CR and non-CR classes. We transform (b) to acquire (d), the Median-Weighted mask (M ) to adaptively down-weight background pixel loss in the Median-Weighted loss function while maintaining the weight for sources at 1.0 regardless of their brightness.
